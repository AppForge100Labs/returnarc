Support

Help with Returnarc, a private habit tracker for iPhone.

Getting started

Create a habit from Today, choose its schedule, and mark it complete when it is due. Progress shows calendar history, streaks, summaries, milestones and a calm return path after a miss.

Reminders

Reminders are local notifications. If one does not appear, check the habit’s reminder setting and the app’s notification permission in iPhone Settings. Turning off notification permission does not delete your habit data.

Widgets, Siri and Shortcuts

Add the widget from the iPhone widget gallery. Open the app after changing habits if a system surface has not refreshed yet. Siri and Shortcuts actions use the habits available from the app’s local projection.

Purchases and restore

Open Settings in the app and choose Restore Purchases if an active Pro subscription is not recognized. Subscription management and cancellation are available from your Apple Account settings. Ending Pro does not delete habits or history; capabilities above the free limit become read-only as described in the app.

Export or delete data

Use Export Data in Settings to create a local copy. Use Delete All Data in Settings to remove the app’s local habit database after confirmation. These actions do not change backups controlled by your Apple settings.

Contact support

Email appforge100@gmail.com. Include the app version, iOS version, device model and steps to reproduce the issue. Do not send private habit names, schedules, completion history, exported data, passwords or payment details.
