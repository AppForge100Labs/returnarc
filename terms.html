Subscriptions

Returnarc Pro is available as an auto-renewable monthly or annual subscription. The price, billing period, trial eligibility and renewal terms shown by Apple at the time of purchase control your transaction.

Payment is charged to your Apple Account. A subscription renews automatically unless it is cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage or cancel subscriptions in your Apple Account settings. Any unused portion of a free trial is forfeited when a subscription is purchased where Apple applies that rule.

Free and Pro access

The free tier supports up to three active habits. Pro supports up to twelve active habits and the additional capabilities described in the app. If Pro ends, Returnarc preserves habits and history; capabilities above the free limit become read-only until you reduce active use or renew.
